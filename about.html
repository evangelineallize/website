I am a freelance designer and illustrator. I was born in France in 1998. I studied for a year at the Atelier de Sèvres in Paris, then completed a degree in McGill University (Montreal, Canada), majoring in Philosophy and minoring in Music and Economics. I am currently the illustration editor at Le Délit, the francophone newspaper of McGill University.
